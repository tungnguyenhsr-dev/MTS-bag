Vietnam is one of the world's leading hubs for bag and backpack manufacturing. This guide explains what an OEM bag manufacturer in Vietnam actually does, what to expect on MOQ and lead time, and how brands like yours can move from a design file to a finished product — using Minh Tùng Studio (MTS) as a concrete example.

What is an OEM bag manufacturer? An OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) bag factory produces bags to your design, techpack, or reference sample. You own the brand; the factory sources materials, builds samples, and runs production. ODM (Original Design Manufacturer) goes one step further — the factory also helps optimize the design and materials. Minh Tùng Studio operates as both an OEM and ODM partner from its closed-loop workshop in Ho Chi Minh City.

Why brands choose a bag factory in Vietnam

Vietnam combines skilled sewing labor, mature textile and hardware supply chains, and competitive production costs. For fashion labels, streetwear brands, and startups, a Vietnamese OEM/ODM partner offers a balance of quality and low minimum order quantities (MOQs) that is hard to match in higher-cost regions.

  • Lower MOQ than mass manufacturers — MTS starts at 300 pieces per SKU, making custom bags viable for small and emerging brands.
  • Closed-loop control — cutting, sewing, branding, QC, and packing happen under one roof, which protects consistency and lead time.
  • Material flexibility — genuine leather, PU leather, canvas, nylon, polyester, and eco options (rPET/cotton/bamboo) are all workable.

Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum order quantity at MTS?

MOQ is 300 pieces per SKU. Volume pricing (8–15% off) applies from 1,000+ pieces, and multi-SKU orders get an additional 5% off the total.

How long does bag production take in Vietnam?

For MTS, production lead time is 10–25 days after sample approval, depending on product type and material. The full cycle including sampling is typically 3–5 weeks.

Do you offer free samples?

Yes. MTS makes a real physical sample from your brief; the sample fee is deducted from your production order once you proceed.

Which bag types can you manufacture?

Backpacks, sling/crossbody bags, totes, duffels/weekenders, and leather accessories (card holders, wallets, pouches, keychains).

Is MTS a trading company or a factory?

MTS is a factory — not a trading intermediary. Cutting, sewing, logo application, QC, and packing are done in-house in Ho Chi Minh City.
