How to Start a Bag Brand in 2026: A Founder's Step-by-Step Guide

Starting a bag brand no longer requires a factory of your own. With low-MOQ OEM/ODM manufacturers in Vietnam, a new brand can go from idea to a real product line with 300 units. This guide walks through the practical steps — and shows where a production partner like Minh Tùng Studio fits in.

Definition: An OEM/ODM bag manufacturer is a factory that produces bags to your brand's design (OEM) or helps develop and produce them (ODM). You focus on brand, design, and customers; the factory handles cutting, sewing, branding, and QC. MTS is a closed-loop OEM/ODM bag factory in Ho Chi Minh City with a 300-piece minimum.

The 7 steps to launch a bag brand

STEP 1

Pick a niche, not "bags"

Vague positioning gets lost. Choose a focused angle — commuter laptop backpacks, eco totes, gym duffels, or minimal crossbody bags. A clear niche shapes your design, pricing, and which factory capabilities you need.

STEP 2

Design & techpack

Create a techpack: dimensions, materials, hardware, pocket layout, and a reference sketch or sample. If you don't have one, an ODM partner can help refine materials and construction to hit your price point.

STEP 3

Choose a manufacturer (MOQ & lead time)

For small brands, MOQ matters most. MTS starts at 300 pieces per SKU with a 10–25 day production lead time after sample approval, and offers a free physical sample so you can verify quality before committing.

STEP 4

Sample & approve

Expect a sample in 7–10 days. Check stitch quality, material feel, hardware function, and logo placement. Approve only when it matches your brief — this sample becomes the production reference.

STEP 5

Production & QC

Your factory runs cutting, sewing, branding, and in-house QC (MTS uses AQL 2.5 per-batch inspection, with photos sent before shipment). A closed-loop workshop keeps consistency high.

STEP 6

Branding & packaging

Decide logo application (emboss, heat transfer, woven label) and packing (retail-ready or ecommerce-standard). MTS packs per Shopee/TikTok standards if you sell online.

STEP 7

Launch & iterate

Sell through your store, marketplaces, or wholesale. Use early feedback to refine the next run — and because MOQ is only 300, you can test variants without over-committing inventory.

Cost levers for a new bag brand

  • Material choice — canvas and PU leather are cost-effective; genuine leather raises both price and perceived value.
  • Construction — an ODM partner can suggest changes that cut 10–20% cost while keeping the look.
  • Volume — MTS pricing drops 8–15% from 1,000+ pieces, plus 5% off for multi-SKU orders.
